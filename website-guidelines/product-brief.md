# Project Brief

## What is it?
A premium, dark-themed, highly tactical landing page for "RATS", an elite and serious clan in the hardcore tactical shooter video game *Squad*. The site acts as both a recruitment funnel for high-quality new members and a central hub for the existing community.

## Who is it for?
- **Primary:** Hardcore, dedicated *Squad* players looking for a serious, organized, and skilled clan to join. They value communication, teamwork, and tactical gameplay.
- **Secondary:** Existing RATS clan members needing a central place to view the roster, rules, and clan information.

## What's the goal?
To convert visitors into Discord members and recruits. The site must immediately establish dominance, professionalism, and a serious militaristic vibe. We want to impress them with a high-end web presence that mirrors the tactical depth of the clan.

## Sections needed
1. Navigation Bar
2. Hero Section
3. About Us
4. Requirements
5. Roster
6. FAQ
7. Footer

## Additional Requirements
- Tech Stack: Next.js + Tailwind CSS
- Fully responsive (mobile and desktop)
- Fast loading with smooth, high-end animations (Framer Motion)
- Dark mode only (militaristic, high contrast)
- Serious, tactical typography
