# Footer Specifications

## Content
- **Logo Area:** `[RATS] SQUAD CLAN`
- **Description:** `Elite tactical operations in Squad. Communication, strategy, lethality.`
- **Links (Columns):**
  - **Column 1:** `Navigation` -> `Home`, `About`, `Roster`, `FAQ`
  - **Column 2:** `Socials` -> `Discord`, `YouTube`, `Twitch`
- **Copyright:** `© 2024 RATS Clan. All rights reserved.`

## Layout Reference
- **Layout:** Standard multi-column layout at the bottom, finishing with a horizontal rule and the copyright text.

## Design Instructions
- **Background:** Pure black or very dark grey (`bg-zinc-950`).
- **Typography:** Monospace fonts for links to keep the tactical theme. Small text size (`text-sm` or `text-xs`).
- **Separation:** A very subtle 1px border at the top of the footer (`border-t border-zinc-900`) to separate it from the last section.
- **Logo:** Keep it large and stark white or yellow.
