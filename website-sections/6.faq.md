# FAQ Specifications

## Content
- **Section Tag:** `04 // INTELLIGENCE`
- **Main Headline:** `FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS`
- **Sub-Headline:** `Need intel before committing? Read our standard operating procedures.`
- **FAQ Items:**
  1. **Q:** `What timezones do you operate in?` - **A:** `We primarily operate in NA (EST/PST) and EU timezones with dedicated squads for both.`
  2. **Q:** `Do I need DLC or specific mods?` - **A:** `We play vanilla Squad, but occasionally participate in modded events. We will announce any required mods well in advance.`
  3. **Q:** `How does the recruitment process work?` - **A:** `Join our Discord, submit an application, and you will be invited to a trial operation where we assess your communication and teamwork.`
  4. **Q:** `Are you a competitive/esports clan?` - **A:** `Yes. While we enjoy casual pub-stomping, our primary focus is structured, competitive 50v50 matches and tournaments.`

## Layout Reference
- **Layout:** An accordion or standard stacked list of questions that expand to reveal answers.

## Design Instructions
- **Accordion:** Clean, border-bottom separated items. The question text should be bold and white. An arrow or plus icon indicating expandability should be on the right.
- **Expansion:** When expanded, the answer should be grey (`text-zinc-400`).
- **Accent:** On hover or active, the question text turns yellow (`text-[#FFD700]`).

## Animations / Effects
- **Expansion:** Smooth height and opacity animation (`AnimatePresence` in Framer Motion) when revealing the answer.
