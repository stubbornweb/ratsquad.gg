# Navigation Bar Specifications

## Content
- **Logo Area:** `[RATS]` or Clan Icon.
- **Links:** 
  - `ABOUT US`
  - `REQUIREMENTS`
  - `ROSTER`
  - `FAQ`
- **Call to Action (CTA):** `JOIN DISCORD`

## Layout Reference
- **Style:** Fixed/Sticky at the top, full width. Very minimal.
- **Height:** Around `h-16` to `h-20`.

## Design Instructions
- **Background:** Transparent initially, turning into a heavily blurred, slightly darker background (`backdrop-blur-md bg-zinc-950/80`) on scroll.
- **Logo:** Bold, yellow (`text-[#FFD700]`).
- **Links:** White text, hovering to yellow. Uppercase, tracked out slightly (`tracking-widest`).
- **CTA Button:** Solid yellow background with black text (`bg-[#FFD700] text-black`), sharp corners (`rounded-none`). High-contrast hover effect (e.g., inverted colors or a white border).
- **Typography:** Use the mono or uppercase sans-serif font for navigation links to emphasize the military/tactical feel.
