# Requirements Specifications

## Content
- **Section Tag:** `02 // JOIN THE PACK`
- **Main Headline:** `DO YOU HAVE WHAT IT TAKES?`
- **Sub-Headline:** `We are highly selective. Read the criteria below before applying to join the RATS.`
- **Requirements List:**
  1. **Title:** `Age Requirement` - **Desc:** `Must be 18+ years old. No exceptions.`
  2. **Title:** `Microphone` - **Desc:** `A high-quality, clear working microphone is mandatory for communications.`
  3. **Title:** `Experience` - **Desc:** `Minimum of 100 hours in Squad. You need to know the basics before we train you for advanced tactics.`
  4. **Title:** `Activity` - **Desc:** `Must be active in Discord and attend at least one mandatory operation per week.`
  5. **Title:** `Attitude` - **Desc:** `No toxicity, no lone wolves. You follow orders, you give accurate callouts, and you play for the team.`
- **Primary CTA:** `SUBMIT APPLICATION` (Links to Discord/Form)

## Layout Reference
- **Layout:** A centered headline with a vertical list or a staggered grid of requirement cards below it.

## Design Instructions
- **Cards/List Items:** Each requirement should look like a "warning label" or a "mission dossier" file. 
- **Colors:** Deep grey background (`bg-zinc-900`), thin yellow or white top-border to give it an industrial file-folder look.
- **Typography:** The titles should be yellow or stark white, uppercase. The description should be readable grey.
- **CTA:** Centered at the bottom, very prominent, perhaps with a pulsing glow effect to draw attention.

## Animations / Effects
- **Staggered Entrance:** If using a grid/list, the items should fade in one after the other (`staggerChildren` in Framer Motion).
