# About Us Specifications

## Content
- **Section Tag:** `01 // WHO WE ARE`
- **Main Headline:** `FORGED IN COMBAT. UNITED BY STRATEGY.`
- **Body Text:**
  - `RATS is a competitive, communication-first clan operating exclusively in Squad. We are not just a group of casual players; we are a dedicated unit focused on executing high-level infantry, armor, and logistics strategies.`
  - `Founded by veterans of the game, our culture is built on respect, continuous improvement, and the refusal to accept defeat without a fight. When you play with RATS, you are part of a lethal machine.`
- **Key Stats / Features (Grid):**
  - **Stat 1:** `50+` (Active Members)
  - **Stat 2:** `3+` (Weekly Operations)
  - **Stat 3:** `100%` (Comms Enforced)
  - **Stat 4:** `Infantry & Armor` (Core Focus)

## Layout Reference
- **Layout:** Split layout. Left side has the tag, headline, and paragraph text. Right side has a 2x2 grid displaying the "Key Stats".

## Design Instructions
- **Background:** Extremely dark charcoal (`bg-zinc-950`).
- **Stats Grid:** Each stat card should have a 1px border (`border-zinc-800`), dark background (`bg-zinc-900/50`), and sharp corners. The stat number itself should be large, yellow (`text-[#FFD700]`), and perhaps use a monospaced font for a tactical readout look.
- **Typography:** Paragraph text should be muted grey (`text-zinc-400`), very legible. Headline in crisp white.

## Animations / Effects
- **Scroll Reveal:** Elements fade in and slide up slightly as the user scrolls into the section using Framer Motion's `whileInView`.
