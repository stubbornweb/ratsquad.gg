# Hero Section Specifications

## Content
- **Pre-Headline Tag (Small):** `[ SQUAD CLAN // EST. 2024 ]`
- **Main Headline:** `TACTICAL EXCELLENCE. DOMINATE THE FIELD.`
- **Sub-Headline:** `The RATS are an elite, coordinated group in Squad. We prioritize communication, lethal execution, and brotherhood. No lone wolves. Only the pack.`
- **Buttons / CTAs:**
  - **Primary:** `APPLY NOW` (Links to Discord)
  - **Secondary:** `VIEW ROSTER` (Scrolls down)

## Layout Reference
- **Layout:** Centered or left-aligned heavy text over a full-screen, dark, gritty background image (or video loop) from the game *Squad*.

## Components to Use
- **Background:** High-quality image or looping video with a heavy, dark vignette and noise overlay so the white and yellow text pops perfectly.
- **Typography Layout:** Large, imposing headline, very tight line-height to feel solid and blocky.
- **Buttons:** 
  - Primary: Solid yellow, black text, sharp corners.
  - Secondary: Transparent, white border (`border border-zinc-700`), white text, hovering to yellow text and yellow border.

## Animations / Effects
- **Entry Animation:** Use Framer Motion to fade up the tag, headline, sub-headline, and buttons sequentially with a very slight, rigid spring physics (not bouncy, just deliberate).
- **Hover on Primary Button:** A subtle arrow icon slides right, or the background flashes white momentarily.
