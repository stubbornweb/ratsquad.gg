# General Design Moodboard

## Inspiration References
### Reference 1: Azov Brigade Website (azov.org.ua)
- What I like: Extremely high contrast, bold and stark typography, professional militaristic tone, very little clutter. Dark backgrounds with strong, singular accent colors that draw the eye immediately. It feels serious, like a real military organization.
### Reference 2: E-Sports/Tactical Branding (Navi, Vitality)
- What I like: Use of stark Yellow against Black/Charcoal. Yellow screams "caution", "hazard", or "elite" when paired with black. It’s highly visible and memorable.

## Aesthetic Keywords
Tactical, Gritty, Dark, High-Contrast, Professional, Militaristic, Aggressive, Elite, Warning/Hazard.

## Colors
- **Theme:** Dark Mode Only
- **Primary (Accent):** `#FFD700` (Cyber/Tactical Yellow) - Used for primary buttons, active states, key data highlights, and subtle glows. Evokes high-visibility tactical gear, caution tape, and elite status.
- **Secondary (Accent):** `#1E1E1E` (Gunmetal Grey) - Used for card backgrounds and secondary elements.
- **Background:** `#09090B` (Vantablack / Deep Charcoal) - Creates a void-like depth, making the yellow pop and the images look more dramatic.
- **Text (Primary):** `#FAFAFA` (Off-white) - For maximum legibility.
- **Text (Muted):** `#A1A1AA` (Zinc-400) - For secondary descriptions.

## Typography
- **Headlines:** `Space Grotesk` or `Oswald` (Bold, slightly technical, blocky, very serious and commanding).
- **Body:** `Inter` or `Geist` (Clean, highly legible, modern sans-serif).
- **Accent:** Monospaced font like `JetBrains Mono` for tactical data, stats, and small labels (adds a "terminal" or "military HUD" feel).

## Effects & Textures
- **Textures:** Subtle film grain/noise overlay on the background to make it feel gritty and less "digital".
- **Borders:** Hard, 1px solid borders (`#27272A`) on cards and containers. Minimal border radius (0px to 4px max) to keep it feeling industrial and sharp.
- **Hover States:** Instead of soft fades, use sharp, instant color flips (e.g., text turns black, background turns yellow).
- **Images:** High contrast, slightly desaturated, with maybe a subtle yellow duotone or vignette overlay to blend them into the site.
